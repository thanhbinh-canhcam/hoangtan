About

Hi, I'm Hoang Tan, a 2D Artist from Vietnam with a passion for creating stylized game art.

I enjoy bringing ideas to life through illustration, concept art, and visual storytelling. My work is inspired by fantasy games, where I can explore atmosphere, lighting, color, and strong compositions to create memorable images.

Over the years, I've been continuously improving my painting skills through personal studies, freelance projects, and daily practice. I'm always looking for ways to grow, learn from other artists, and challenge myself with new creative work.

I'm currently open to freelance commissions and interested in opportunities within the game industry. Whether it's character art, splash art, or concept art, I aim to create artwork that is both visually engaging and meaningful.
